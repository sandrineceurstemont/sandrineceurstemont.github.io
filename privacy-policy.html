Privacy Policy

Last updated on March 15, 2026.

1. Introduction

This website is a personal website operated by Sandrine Ceurstemont. I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting any personal data you provide when using this website. This privacy policy explains what information may be collected when you visit this website or contact me, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for this website is Sandrine Ceurstemont. Please use the contact form on this website for privacy-related enquiries.

3. Information You Provide

If you contact me through the website’s contact form, you may provide personal information such as your name, your email address and the content of your message. This information is provided voluntarily when you submit the form.

4. How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used only to:

  • Respond to your message or enquiry
  • Communicate with you regarding the subject of your message

I do not use your information for marketing purposes and I do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except as described below.

5. Third-Party Services

The contact form on this website is processed by a third-party service provider called Formspree. When you submit a message, the information you provide may be processed by that service in order to deliver your message. The provider acts as a data processor on my behalf.

6. Legal Basis for Processing

Personal data submitted through the contact form is processed on the basis of legitimate interest, specifically the interest in responding to enquiries and communications initiated by the user. Where you voluntarily provide personal information through the contact form, you acknowledge that this information is necessary in order for me to respond to your request.

7. Website Hosting

This website is hosted using GitHub Pages, a service provided by GitHub, Inc. As part of normal internet operations, GitHub may collect technical information such as IP addresses, browser type and server logs when visitors access the site. I do not access or control these logs directly. For more information, please refer to GitHub’s privacy policy.

8. International Data Transfers

Because the hosting provider and some service providers may operate in the United States or other countries outside the United Kingdom, your information may be processed outside the UK. Where this occurs, service providers are expected to use appropriate safeguards to protect personal data.

8. Data Retention

Personal data submitted through the contact form is kept only for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry or maintain correspondence, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

9. Your Rights Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation and the Data Protection Act 2018

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data I hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing

If you believe your data has been handled improperly, you may also lodge a complaint with the UK’s supervisory authority, the Information Commissioner’s Office.

10. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics tools, advertising trackers, or other tracking technologies.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, you can contact me using the contact form on this website.

12. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
